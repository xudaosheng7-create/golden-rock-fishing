# 游钓天下 BGM 创作 Prompt

## 中文需求

一首轻音乐背景音乐，用于海钓游戏。传达快乐、轻松的情绪。有大海、鸟叫、春暖花开的感觉。乐器以木吉他、钢琴为主，辅以轻弦乐。节奏舒缓但不沉闷，旋律轻快上扬。

---

## English Prompt for AI Music Generator

> Compose a light, cheerful background music track for a mobile fishing game called "Ocean Fishing". The music should evoke the feeling of a sunny spring morning by the sea — gentle ocean waves, distant seabird calls, warm breeze, flowers blooming along the coastline.
>
> **Mood**: Happy, relaxed, peaceful, warm, optimistic
> **Tempo**: Moderato, around 80-90 BPM
> **Key**: C major or G major (bright, open)
> **Duration**: 2-3 minutes, seamless loop
>
> **Instrumentation**:
> - Lead: Nylon-string acoustic guitar (fingerpicking, melodic)
> - Second lead: Soft piano (gentle arpeggios, light chords)
> - Pad: Warm string ensemble (legato, subtle background)
> - Occasional: Soft flute or melodica (seaside whistle feel)
> - Percussion: Very light — soft shaker, gentle cajon, ocean drum
>
> **Sound Effects Layer** (natural, mixed low in background):
> - Gentle waves lapping on shore (continuous, subtle)
> - Distant seagull calls (sparse, every 15-30 seconds)
> - Occasional fishing rod cast / splash (very subtle)
>
> **Structure**:
> - Intro (0:00-0:15): Waves + guitar solo, setting the seaside scene
> - A section (0:15-0:45): Main melody on guitar, piano enters
> - B section (0:45-1:15): Strings swell gently, slightly more emotional
> - A return (1:15-1:45): Back to main melody, flute joins
> - Bridge (1:45-2:15): Key change or variation, warm climax
> - Outro (2:15-2:30): Guitar fades, waves remain, seamless loop back to intro
>
> **Reference Style**: Similar to "Departure" from the Rurouni Kenshin OST, Studio Ghibli seaside scenes, or the gentle acoustic work of Tommy Emmanuel's ballads — but lighter and more game-friendly.
>
> **Technical**: High-quality stereo, 44.1kHz, no clipping, -14 LUFS integrated loudness. Loop point must be seamless (outro flows perfectly back into intro).
>
> The feeling should be: you're standing on a wooden pier at sunrise, rod in hand, coffee warm, the world just waking up, and anything is possible.
